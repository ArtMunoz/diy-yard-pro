Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DIY Yard Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.diyyardpro.com (the "Site").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Leave comments on articles
  • Contact us through our contact form
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

This may include: name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and access times
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device (see Section 5 below)
  • Device Information: Hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Site Operation: To provide, maintain, and improve our Site and services
  • Communication: To send newsletters, updates, and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our Site and improve user experience
  • Advertising: To display personalized advertisements through third-party services
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4.2 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze Site traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information anonymously and report website trends. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4.3 Affiliate Programs

We participate in affiliate marketing programs, including Amazon Associates, Home Depot, and Lowe's. When you click on affiliate links and make purchases, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. These third parties may use cookies to track conversions.

4.4 Social Media Platforms

Our Site may include social media features (e.g., Facebook, Pinterest, Instagram). These features may collect your IP address and set cookies. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policies of the respective platforms.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our Site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand site usage.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Site to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our Site
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect Site functionality.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist in Site operation (e.g., hosting, analytics, email services)
  • Advertising Partners: To display personalized advertisements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition of our business

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Cookie Control: Manage cookie preferences through browser settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@diyyardpro.com

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

Our Site is operated in the United States. If you access our Site from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S., where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:

  • The right to know what personal information is collected
  • The right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • The right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to equal service and price

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@diyyardpro.com

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@diyyardpro.com
  • Website: www.diyyardpro.com
  • Address: [Your Business Address]
