My Story

I've been working in the landscaping industry for over 10 years, and it's been an incredible journey. I didn't start as a designer or business owner‚ÄîI started from the ground up, literally.

I began my career as a laborer, doing the hard physical work that landscaping demands. But instead of just going through the motions, I watched, learned, and asked questions. I wanted to understand why we did things a certain way, how different materials worked together, and what made some projects last decades while others failed in a few years.

Over the years, I learned every aspect of the trade‚Äîfrom basic lawn maintenance to complex hardscaping projects. I worked on stone patios, retaining walls, irrigation systems, outdoor living spaces, and everything in between. Every project taught me something new, and every mistake (yes, there were plenty!) made me better at what I do.

Why DIY Yard Pro?

Here's the thing: I've seen both sides of the landscaping world. I've worked on high-end projects where money wasn't an issue, and I've talked to countless homeowners who desperately wanted to improve their yards but couldn't afford professional services.

The reality is that hiring a professional landscaping company is expensive. A simple patio can cost $5,000-15,000. A full backyard transformation? Easily $20,000-50,000 or more. For most families, that's just not realistic.

But here's what I learned: You don't need to be a professional to create something amazing. What you need is:

  • The right information (which is often hard to find or buried in technical jargon)
  • Step-by-step guidance from someone who's actually done it
  • Tips to avoid expensive mistakes
  • The confidence to tackle a project yourself

That's exactly what I want to provide with DIY Yard Pro. I've seen so many people intimidated by projects that are totally within their capabilities. They just need that initial push‚Äîthat "empujoncito"‚Äîto get started. And once they do, they realize it's not as scary as it seemed.

My Philosophy

I believe that everyone deserves a beautiful outdoor space, regardless of their budget. You shouldn't have to choose between your dream backyard and your kids' college fund.

DIY isn't about being cheap‚Äîit's about being smart. It's about:

  • Learning valuable skills that you'll use for life
  • Taking pride in something you built yourself
  • Saving thousands of dollars on projects you can absolutely handle
